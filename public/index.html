A timber deck on a pre-war Queenslander had three rotted boards, loose balustrade posts, and weathered timber throughout. A homeowner preparing to sell had accumulated years of scuffs, door-handle holes, and hairline cracks across several rooms. We patched eight plaster holes, repaired corner damage on two walls, matched the existing paint colour, and touched up throughout. The result looked freshly repainted and helped the property present at its best on open home day.
